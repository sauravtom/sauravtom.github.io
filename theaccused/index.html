Book Review- Theodore Boone The Accused by John Grisham


Published on 11 AM Tuesday 02 July 2013

book-review




Just like other John Grisham books, this one is also a legal thriller. But unlike others its based on the life of a little boy, and aspiring laywer. Its very captivating and you just keep on turning pages, until you finish the 300 paged book. I finished it in a morning and an afternoon.

[Spoiler] Theodore Boone is a school kid, who come from a family mainly consisting of laywers, with his mother, father and uncle in the legal profession. The whole story is that, her mother is handling a devorce case of the parents of some kids who go to the same school as boone, and as usual she sides with the mother, which makes the kids angry. So they decide to take revenge with boone, by making his life miserable.

Thats it.
