Introducing Euclid


Published on 06 PM Tuesday 3rd November 2015

hackathon




This weekend I participated in the block chain university hackathon and build project Euclid. It is a tool to certify files(media/images) via the blockchain and also a platform for content creators to recieve passive donations for their work via cryptocurrencies.

.@sauravtom presenting "Euclid: certifying files with the blockchain" #blockhack pic.twitter.com/rCmYlFyUOz

— Tim Swanson (@ofnumbers) November 1, 2015


It came third in the hackathon because I was only able to build a website in the 2 days time. Euclid will make the most sense in the form of a mobile app, which I plan to work on soon.

#blockhack 3rd Prize Split BTW: 1) Hashwage w/ @bitwage & @coinbase by @c0rv0s 2) Euclid w/ @BitGo by @sauravtom pic.twitter.com/A6uiOJ9fQO

— BlockchainUniversity (@blockchainU) November 2, 2015


Won a Bitcoin and a whole bunch of hackathon swag at Blockchain University Hackathon today with project euclid (image attribution via blockchain) My US trip is now finally cash positive :D
