Ender's Game book review

Published on 10 AM Tuesday 11 February 2014

book-review

Ender's Game is a science fiction book written by Orson Scott Card, in 1985, this book is about distant future of human civilisation when they encounter an alien specier , 'bugger'.

I read the book after watching the movie, and there is just so much more in the book, not just in terms of depth and details, but also in terms of story and plot.

For example it was never mentioned in the movie that, Valentine and Peter were creating chaos on the nets, as Demosthenes and Locke, which is a quite vital piece of information, and was needed to justify the on-screen real estate of Valentice (Ender's Sister).

The book is brilliant piece of science fiction. Go give it a read, you will love it.
