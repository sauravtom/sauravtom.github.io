Abyss - a poem


Published on 06 AM Thursday 08 January 2015

general,poem




Miles and miles away you stay
The farther, the glitzier flash,
For you are a star in my universe
Tell me if I'm being selfish here?
I want this star in my jewellery box.

I assume you are a make-believe cosmic
You are here but never near
Still I tell you, you are so dear
You definitely can not chide me
Through my window frame, you appear scintillating

You say we are a bunch of monkeys
Riding a space rock in abyss of space
So here, we cohere through interface
And wish that our friendship never fades.
In a parallel universe somewhere , I believe
We are shining bright together.
